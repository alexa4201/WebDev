Frequently Asked Quetsions

Text.

Where can I find my Road Map and the General Education Core Curriculum?

You can access all UTRGV Road Maps here, and the General Education Core Curriculum will be found here. Note: Some majors may need to take specific courses from the GED.

Where can I find this building?

In this link you will find maps for all UTRGV campuses. Note: If the classroom number is "2.203", then the classroom will be in the second floor. If it where "1.103", then it would be the first floor.

Who is my advisor?

You can find your advisor by doing the following
1. Log in to my.utrgv.edu
2. Under Applications you will select EAB Navigate
3. On your right you will find your Advisor under Your Success Team.

How do I schedule an advising session?

1. Log in to my.utrgv.edu
2. Under Applications you will select EAB Navigate
3. On your right you will find the Get Assistance button, click on it and beging scheduling the type of assistance you need.

How can I find what textbooks I will need?

You can do this by going to The University Bookstore page. All you have to do is type in your Student ID. Note: Always check your UTRGV email, professors may change or request additional materials.

How should I prepare for my Advising Session?

  • Review your Degree Works on Assist
  • Write down any questions you might have
  • Create two or three Course Schedule Options
  • Check for any holds
